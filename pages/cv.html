Documentary photographer and journalist.
Born in 1991 in Molodeczno, Belarus, now baised in Warsaw, Poland.

Since october 2020 — Chief of Photo Department at Belsat TV (the only Belarusian language independent television channel).

In her works Julia focuses on social themes. She has been documenting the lives of people with disabilities in Belarus. For the past few years Julia has been focusing on the topic of equality in sport, since 2018 she has been documenting the women's team of the Warsaw alternative sports club “Zły”.

Almost all the author's works are long-term projects. Julia's works were presented at exhibitions in Belarus, Poland and Lithuania.

In 2020, Julia was included on the list of 30 under 30 woman photographers by ARTPIL. Published in CalvertJournal, Esquire Russia, National Geographic Poland, PHmuseum, Bird In Flight, FK Magazine and ImenaMag.
